About Us

APFusion is a Champaign, IL-based venture capital-backed hyper-growth startup. We’re a technology company focused on changing the automotive industry forever. APFusion has developed a solution that helps solve the communication gap in the automotive industry and is bringing together aftermarket companies and automotive salvage yards like never before. Through the APFusion marketplace, salvage yards get access to tens of millions of dollars worth of inventory while our vendors get unparalleled distribution power.

APFusion is one of those rare companies that have the opportunity to drive an underserved antiquated industry forward. We get the unique privilege to move the technology needle forward within the automotive salvage industry.

Mission Statement

We believe that the automotive salvage industry has been overlooked and brushed off as a “dusty old industry.”

We want to change that.

The tools provided for the every day automotive salvage yard are clunky and inefficient. It’s time to bring world class technology to this underserved industry. Moving the automotive salvage industry forward is at the heart of every decision we make.

There is a difference between a focus on customer experience and a focus on customer satisfaction. Customer experience is how the customer interacts with the product day to day. Customer satisfaction is how the customer feels about the product that they interact with. The higher the customer satisfaction levels, the higher the engagement.
